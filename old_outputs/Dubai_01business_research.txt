**A Detailed Research Report on Top 5 Businesses in Dubai**

This report details five prominent businesses operating in Dubai.  Information is based on publicly available data and may not represent a completely exhaustive profile of each company.

**1. Emirates Airline:**

*   **Company History:** Founded in 1985, Emirates is the flagship airline of the United Arab Emirates, based in Dubai. It has grown from a small operation to become one of the world's largest airlines. [18, 19]
*   **Operations:** Emirates operates a vast network of international flights, connecting Dubai to over 150 destinations across six continents.  It's known for its modern fleet and high-quality service. [18, 19]
*   **Industry:** Airlines and Aviation
*   **Notable Achievements:**  Emirates has received numerous awards for its service and operational excellence.  Its rapid growth and global reach are significant achievements. [18]


**2. Etisalat:**

*   **Company History:**  Etisalat (now rebranded as e&) is a large telecommunications company founded in 1976. It has expanded significantly to become a major player in the Middle East and beyond. [20, 21]
*   **Operations:** Etisalat provides a wide range of telecommunications services, including mobile, fixed-line, internet, and other related offerings. [20, 21]
*   **Industry:** Telecommunications
*   **Notable Achievements:** Etisalat's success lies in its market dominance in the UAE and its regional expansion, servicing millions of customers. [20]


**3. ADNOC:**

*   **Company History:** The Abu Dhabi National Oil Company (ADNOC) is one of the world's leading oil and gas companies. Its history is intertwined with the development of the UAE.  [22]
*   **Operations:** ADNOC engages in exploration, production, refining, and distribution of oil and gas.  It plays a vital role in the UAE economy. [22]
*   **Industry:** Oil and Gas
*   **Notable Achievements:** ADNOC's significant contribution to the UAE's economy and its global presence as a major oil and gas player are notable achievements. [22]


**4. Emirates NBD:**

*   **Company History:** Emirates NBD is a major banking institution in the UAE, formed through a merger of two prominent banks. [23]
*   **Operations:**  Emirates NBD offers a wide range of financial services, including retail banking, corporate banking, and investment banking. [23]
*   **Industry:** Banking and Finance
*   **Notable Achievements:** Emirates NBD's role as a leading financial institution in Dubai and its strong performance in the market are significant achievements. [23]
